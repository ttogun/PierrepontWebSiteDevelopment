2013 NATIONAL LATIN EXAM RESULTS

Latin I-V Exams. Of the thirty students at this level, twenty-one were awarded Gold Medals and Summa Cum Laude certificates, the highest award. Three were awarded Silver Medals and Maxima Cum Laude certificates, the second highest award. Two received a Magna Cum Laude certificate and three a certificate of Cum Laude.

Introduction to Latin Exam. Of the twenty students at this level, five earned perfect scores, and were awarded Ribbons and Perfect Paper certificates, the highest award for this level of test. Eleven students were awarded Ribbons and certificates of Outstanding Achievement, the second highest award. Two students were awarded certificates of Achievement.
